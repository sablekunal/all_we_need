Overview

Unmute.sh is a demo platform by Kyutai showcasing their low-latency open-source audio foundation models. It enables real-time, natural spoken conversations with AI.

Features

  • Real-time Interaction: Extremely low latency speech processing.
  • End-to-End Audio: Speech-to-text and text-to-speech integration.
  • Open Source: Based on Kyutai's open research and models.
  • Kyutai Labs: Developed by the Kyutai research lab.
